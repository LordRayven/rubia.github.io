Types of Dogs

Golden Retriever

The Golden Retriever is a friendly, intelligent, and devoted dog breed.

Labrador Retriever

The Labrador Retriever is one of the most popular dog breeds known for their friendly and outgoing nature.

German Shepherd

The German Shepherd is a highly intelligent and versatile working dog known for its loyalty and protectiveness.

Bulldog

The Bulldog is a medium-sized breed known for its loose, wrinkled skin and distinctive pushed-in nose.

Beagle

The Beagle is a small to medium-sized hound breed known for its friendly and curious nature.

Poodle

The Poodle is an intelligent and highly trainable breed known for its hypoallergenic coat.

Dachshund

The Dachshund is a small breed with a long body and short legs, known for its playful and lively personality.

Siberian Husky

The Siberian Husky is a medium-sized working dog breed known for its striking blue eyes and thick double coat.

Pug

The Pug is a small breed known for its wrinkled face and curled tail. They are charming, affectionate, and have a playful personality.

Chihuahua

The Chihuahua is a balanced, graceful dog of terrier-like demeanor, weighing no more than 6 pounds..
